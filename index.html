About Us

GreenStream Solutions is dedicated to e-waste management.

We are a community-driven NGO focused on safe e-waste collection, recycling, and education. Our programs create local jobs, reduce hazardous waste, and help communities transition to more sustainable technology practices.

Our Vision

A world where technology is circular and electronic waste never harms communities.

Our Mission

Collect, recycle and educate—while creating sustainable livelihoods.

Our Programs

Practical projects that turn e-waste into opportunity.

E-waste Collection & Recycling

We organize collection drives and partner with certified recyclers to process electronics safely.

Education & Awareness

Workshops, school programs and campaigns to raise awareness on safe disposal and reuse.

Corporate Partnerships

Helping businesses set up responsible take-back and e-waste management policies.

Policy Advocacy

Advocating for stronger regulations and incentives for circular electronics.

Sustainable Tech Innovations

Pilots for repair hubs, refurbishing and modular design to extend product life.

Community Collection Hubs

Local drop-off points and pick-up schedules tailored to neighborhoods.
